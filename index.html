ABOUT US

ORIONTEL EXIM is a general trading and import-export company registered with SECP under the Companies Act, 2017. Established in June 2025, we are authorized to import and export all kinds of goods, raw materials, and finished products, making us a trusted partner in local and global trade.

We specialize in trade representation, commercial services, and value-added trading across multiple industries.

OUR VISION

To be a global trading hub that connects markets and creates sustainable growth.

OUR MISSION

Deliver efficient, compliant, and innovative trading solutions built on trust, integrity, and long-term partnerships.

WHAT WE DO

General Trading & Distribution

Buy, sell, and distribute lawful goods across Pakistan through wholesale, retail, and online channels.

Trade & Commercial Representation

Represent local and international brands as their trusted partner, promoting products and expanding market reach.

Procurement & Supply Solutions

Offer sourcing, procurement, and logistics services to support manufacturers, importers and clients.

E-Commerce & Digital Trade

Develop and manage online stores, market places and digital trade channels to reach wider audiences.

Value-Added Trading Services

We import quality products, refine and rebrand them for stronger market appeal, and provide reliable local delivery with full after-sales care, warranty and certified dealership support.
