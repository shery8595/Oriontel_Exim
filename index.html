About Us

ORIONTEL EXIM is a forward-thinking import-export and trade facilitation company dedicated to bridging global markets with local excellence. Founded on the principles of integrity, innovation, and efficiency, we provide streamlined, transparent, and compliant solutions for cross-border commerce.

Vision

To become a trusted global gateway for authorized commodities and personalized trade solutions, committed to excellence, regulatory compliance, and lasting customer trust.

Mission

  • Deliver seamless, fully compliant import-export solutions
  • Build enduring partnerships grounded in transparency
  • Empower local businesses with global opportunities
  • Harness modern technology for smarter trade operations

Our Services

Authorized Commodity Trade

Import and export of licensed and approved goods in accordance with Pakistani and international trade laws.

Tailored Client Services

Custom trade and logistics solutions with full-cycle support: sourcing, procurement, documentation, and shipping.

Digital Trade Support

Online facilitation of trade operations with real-time shipment tracking and electronic documentation.

Advisory & Compliance
