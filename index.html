ABOUT US

ORIONTEL EXIM (SMC-Private) Limited is your trusted partner in modern trade solutions, seamlessly connecting Pakistani businesses with international markets. Registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP) since June 12, 2025, we specialize in end-to-end import, export, and domestic trade services.

Driven by legal compliance, operational integrity, and global best practices, we deliver efficient and transparent trade solutions across borders and within Pakistan.

OUR VISION

To become the world’s most trusted trade gateway — powered by technology, compliance, and unmatched operational excellence.

OUR MISSION

  • Simplify global trade through seamless, regulation-compliant import/export and domestic solutions.
  • Forge lasting partnerships through transparency, reliability, and performance.
  • Empower Pakistani businesses to compete and thrive in international markets.
  • Lead innovation in digital trade infrastructure and sustainable practices.

OUR SERVICES

Authorized Commodity Trade

  • Licensed import/export of all SECP-permissible goods
  • Legally compliant domestic trading across Pakistan

Tailored End-to-End Solutions

Digital Trade Hub

  • Strategic trade consulting (domestic & international)
